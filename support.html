Support¶

If you could not find the answer to your question in the the docs you are welcome to send it to the users mailing list hyperspy-users@googlegroups.com or start a conversation in the gitter chat (here or by clicking “Open chat” the lower right-hand corner of the window).

If you want to join the mailing list without a Google account send an email to hyperspy-users+subscribe@googlegroups.com. There is also a web-based version of this list but you need to have a Google account to use it.

If you think that you have found a bug or you would like to request a feature, please create a new issue in the issues tracker.

You may also be interested in joining us for any forthcoming training Trainings or in inviting us to organize one.
