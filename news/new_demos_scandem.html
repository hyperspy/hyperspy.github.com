New demos added¶

Published on 2016-06-17 00:00:00

The existing demos have been updated and three new demos have been added.

The new demos are:

  • Getting started with HyperSpy

  • HyperSpy fitting tutorial

  • EELS analysis tutorial

The demos are in the form of IPython notebooks and contain examples of usage. They can be viewed online and downloaded for interactive execution.

Everybody is encouraged to contribute demos by making a pull request to the hyperspy-demos github repository or by email to the hyperspy users mailing list.
