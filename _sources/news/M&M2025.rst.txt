HyperSpy Workshop at M&M 2025
=============================

We are happy to announce a HyperSpy workshop at the `Microscopy and Microanalysis 2025 (M&M 2025) <https://microscopy.org/past-m-m-meetings>`_. The workshop will take place online on the 27th of July 2025.

Teaching materials and agenda will be available closer to the date.
