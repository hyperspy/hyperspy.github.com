HyperSpy Tutorial at DPG Spring Meeting 2026
============================================

We are happy to announce a HyperSpy tutorial at the `Spring Meeting of the German Physical Society (DPG 2026) <https://dresden26.dpg-tagungen.de>`_. The in-person tutorial session will be part of the meeting of the DPG's Condensed Matter Section (SKM) in Dresden and is scheduled for Sunday, March 8, 2026 from 16:00 to 18:15. It will include a short intro to HyperSpy and four hands-on sessions dedicated to the general usage of `HyperSpy <https://hyperspy.org/hyperspy-doc/current>`_, as well as the extensions `lumiSpy <https://lumispy.org>`_, `eXSpy <https://hyperspy.org/exspy>`_ and `pyxem <https://pyxem.readthedocs.io>`_.

The agenda and more information will be available  `at the conference website <https://dresden26.dpg-tagungen.de/programme/tutorials>`_ from January 30, 2026. Teaching materials will be published by March 1, 2026, on a dedicated `GitHub repositorium <https://github.com/LumiSpy/DPG2026-Tutorial>`_.
